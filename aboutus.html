ABOUT US





Capstone Cloud Services are an SME cloud service provider. The aim of our business is to provide quality & efficient services for to meet varying demands, providing personal & corporate level cloud services to users who possess little to no experience of the cloud.


Our Web Hosting service allows users to host a website on Capstone Cloud. The service is powered by AWS EC2. The service is similar to GoDaddy or Wix.


The Bespoke Package Service is consultancy-based service offered by the company. This can include anything from helping a customer design a website, to hosting complex websites on Capstone Cloud, or storing sensitive or high-risk data.
